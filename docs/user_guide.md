# BrandForge User Guide: From Strategy to Design

Welcome to **BrandForge**, your absolute branding command center. This guide is designed for non-technical users to help you navigate the platform and forge a world-class brand identity in record time.

---

## 🔑 1. Accessing Your Workspace

### Signing In
- **Email & Password**: Enter your registered credentials to access your persistent project library.
- **Google Sign-In**: Use your existing Google account for one-click access.
- **First-Time Users**: If you are new, your first log-in will automatically initialize an empty "Commander Console" for you to start building.

### The Commander Console (Dashboard)
Your dashboard is where you manage your active projects.
- **Start New**: Click the **"New Project"** button to trigger the Branding Blueprint.
- **Search**: Use the top search bar to find projects by name or client.
- **View Toggle**: Switch between **Grid View** (visual cards) and **List View** (compact rows) depending on your workflow depth.

---

## 🧠 2. Connecting the Brain (AI Connectivity)

BrandForge uses advanced AI to synthesize your brand strategy. For now, you can leverage your preferred **Free AI Accounts** to power the suite.

### How to Connect Your Free Keys:
1. Open the **Settings Console** (the gear icon ⚙️ in the sidebar).
2. Navigate to the **"AI Settings"** tab.
3. **Gemini (Google)**: 
   - Visit the [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey) to generate a free API key.
   - Paste it into the **Gemini API Key** field.
4. **OpenAI (ChatGPT/DALL-E)**:
   - Visit the [OpenAI Platform](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys) to generate a key.
   - Paste it into the **OpenAI API Key** field.
5. **Save**: The platform will now use these keys to generate strategies, nouns, and visual concepts.

## 🧭 3. The 5-Step Branding Workflow

### Step 1: Discovery (The Branding Blueprint)
- **Goal**: Establish the foundational DNA of the brand.
- **How to Use**: Follow the 9-phase progress stepper. Answer questions about your vision, values, and audience.
- **Tip**: You can "Import from Google Forms" if you’ve already collected data from a client.

### Step 2: Strategy (The Soul)
- **Goal**: Define the brand's personality.
- **How to Use**: Once Discovery is complete, click **"Forge Strategy."** The AI will generate a **Carl Jung Archetype** profile, Positioning Statement, and Messaging Framework.
- **Action**: Review and click **"Approve Strategy"** to lock in the logic.

### Step 3: Logo Assistant (The Alchemist)
- **Goal**: Translate strategy into visual ideas.
- **How to Use**: 
   - **Noun Toolkit**: Generate linguistic concepts based on your strategy.
   - **Concept Smushes**: Combine two nouns (e.g., "Shield" + "Feather") to create a unique logo concept.
   - **Pd Scanner**: Check the **Propositional Density** to ensure your logo idea is both simple and meaningful.

### Step 4: Brand System (Visual Language)
- **Goal**: Set the rules for how the brand looks.
- **How to Use**: Define your color palettes, typography pairings, and grid systems. The platform will automatically suggest styles that match your "Strategic Vibe."

### Step 5: Handoff (The Export Center)
- **Goal**: Get your brand out into the world.
- **How to Use**: Click **"Download PDF"** in the Strategy or Logo tools to generate a high-fidelity Blueprint. Use the **"Generate Brand Guide"** tool for a full-scale digital manual.

## ⚙️ 4. The Settings Console De-Mystified

- **General**: Set your workspace name and default branding preferences.
- **Account**: Manage your profile and security.
- **Notifications**: Access the **Notification Audit Center** to see a history of all project changes and messages.
- **AI Configuration**: Toggle between Gemini and OpenAI models and manage your free keys.
- **Data & Portability**: Download your entire project library as a backup or import a previous project file.
- **Appearance**: Toggle between the "Modern Blueprint" light mode and a focus-driven Dark Mode.

---

## 📖 5. Key Terms for Non-Brander's
- **Propositional Density (Pd)**: The ratio of "Meaning" to "Visual Complexity." Simple logos with deep meaning have high Pd.
- **Archetype**: A universal personality type (e.g., The Explorer, The Caregiver) used to make a brand feel human.
- **S.I.P (Sequential Intelligence Pipeline)**: The technology that ensures your Discovery answers actually influence your Logo and Design System.
